## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **Hookshot** (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile game.

Hookshot is a simple 2D pixel-art arcade game built with LibGDX. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency. This policy is written in plain English and is intended to be globally applicable.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

Hookshot **does not require user accounts** and **does not collect personal information directly** such as your name or email address.

The App uses **Firebase Analytics** to collect **limited, anonymous analytics data**, which may include:

* App interaction events (e.g., session start, gameplay events)
* Device type and operating system version
* App instance identifiers
* Performance and usage metrics
* Approximate location derived from IP address (only if enabled by Firebase)

This data is collected automatically through the analytics SDK and is not used to identify you personally.

---

## 3. Information We Do Not Collect

We explicitly do **not** collect:

* Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
* User account information (no login or registration)
* Precise location data (GPS)
* Contacts or address book data
* Camera or microphone data
* User-generated content or chat messages
* Payment or financial information

The App contains **no ads** and **no in-app purchases**.

---

## 4. How We Use Information

We use analytics data solely to:

* Improve game mechanics and balance
* Understand general gameplay behavior
* Identify bugs and performance issues
* Improve overall player experience

We do **not** use data for advertising, marketing, or profiling users.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

Hookshot uses the following third-party service:

### Firebase Analytics (Google LLC)

Firebase Analytics helps us understand how the App is used. Data collected by Firebase is processed in accordance with Google’s privacy policies.

* Service provider: Google LLC
